ABOUT CONFERENZA

Conferenza is the Guest Lecture module of our grand annual techno-management fest Tecnoesis which aims at motivating and providing guidance to students by organizing a series of Guest Lectures by a variety of individuals who have achieved remarkable feats in their life and have struggled through with all their might to emerge as winners.

We have hosted various dignitaries in the past like:- IPS Abhyanand (co-founder of Super 30), Balaji Viswanathan (famous Quora writer), IAS Vivek Atray, Dr. Uddhabh Bharali (Inventor aka 'Real Life Phunsukh Wangdu'), IAS Chanchal Rana (AIR 7 UPSC Civil Services Examination), Jadav Payeng ('Forest Man of India'), Rahul Kaushik (Founder & Writer at 'The Melting Words') and the list goes on and on....

Shivankit Singh Parihar

Born and brought up in Satna, Madhya Pradesh, he had been very passionate about films and acting since his childhood. He got his first break with Shuruaat Ka Interval. He is an actor and writer, known for TSP's Rabish Ki Report, Cubicles, Angry Indian, and many more.

His works in comedy TV-Mini-Series, “TVF Bachelors” and “The Screen Patti,” have also received critically acclaimed reviews and love from the audience.

Chetan Cheetah

On February 14, 2017, CRPF troops were conducting a search operation in Bandipora (Kashmir Valley) when terrorists hiding in the area started firing at the troops. But Chetan Kumar Cheetah, Commandant of 45 batallion, bravely stalled the terrorists & ended up in suffering multiple gunshots.

He suffered bullet injuries in his brain, right eye, abdomen, both arms, left hand and in the buttock region while fighting terrorists. He remained in coma for almost 1.5 months before he regained consciousness.
Cheetah also received Kirti Chakra, the second highest peacetime gallantry medal.

Chetan Cheetah says, "I want youngsters to give 100 percent to the country, that's what I have done. The duty which I had, I could have escaped, but instead, I faced the bullets."

Padma Shri Dr. Ravi Kannan

“We make efforts to offer the best treatment possible to all the patients, irrespective of their socio-economic status. No one should be denied access to treatment due to want of money,” Dr. Kannan says.

Padma Shri Dr. Ravi Kannan, senior surgical oncologist, Cachar Cancer Hospital, Silchar, our 4rth Conferenza speaker, a legend in the field of medical sciences quit his job in Chennai in the year 2007 and shifted to Barak Valley, seeing the poor cancer treatment facilities in the valley. Over the years, his drive to provide the best medical treatment to patients irrespective of their socio-economic status has witnessed several innovative measures. Some of these include 'Home Visits' and 'Satellite Clinics' that bring medical care to the doorstep of patients, who are mostly daily wage workers. His journey started with a visit to the Cachar Cancer Hospital in Silchar on request. As he went around the wards of the hospital, the pain of the patients made him quit his practice in Chennai to join the hospital for voluntary service. Over the years, he and his incredible team managed to turn the place around. There were only 23 staff members when he joined. Today, there is a 200-member strong team.

As a matter of fact, Dr. Kannan has treated around 70,000 cancer patients free of cost till now. He has spent 13 years of his life taking cancer treatment to the doorsteps of poor patients, improving follow-up care and subsidising medical costs, and this earned him the fourth most prestigious civilian award in India, Padma Shri, this Republic Day.
